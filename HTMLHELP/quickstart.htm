Quick start

To get you familiar with the program in no time at all, please note the following:

  1. Ensure that your time zone is correctly set up. Do this by choosing Date/Time in the control panel and check your time zone.
  2. To display a location, choose 'Add Location to display' button from the toolbar
  3. Left click on a location to make it the new base reference from which hour differences are calculated A house icon will be displayed to let you know this is your new temporary 'Home' Left click on Home for normal operation. Home is your home location with the yellow border around it
  4. Right click on a location to modify, delete, set alarms or move a location to another position in the display
  5. To exit the program, choose Exit from the menu, or press X. If you close or minimize the window, WorldTime2000 will still be running in the system tray ( bottom right of screen ). Double click the icon to restore the window, or right click the icon for options
