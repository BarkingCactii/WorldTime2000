Adobe Acrobat Reader 4.0 Released Sunday, Apr. 04 - 03:56 PM EDT

     Acrobat Reader 4.0 is an essential tool for anyone who needs to view, navigate, browse, and print Adobe Portable Document Format (PDF) files. The final Acrobat Reader 4.0 software is currently available in English for Windows and Macintosh users. Final software will soon be available for 11 other languages and for UNIX. Acrobat Reader 4.0 with Search will be available on the Acrobat Reader 4.0 CD-ROM. Version 4.0 has a ton of new features, including:

  • Improved integration with Web browsers
  • Faster and more reliable printing to both PCL and Adobe PostScript(R) printers
  • Better support for longer and more complex documents
  • Support for the latest version of the Acrobat file format - Adobe PDF 1.3
  • Support for all versions of PostScript, including the new PostScript 3
  • Better color handling, including support for ICC profiles
  • Compatible with previous versions of Acrobat Reader

WorldTime 2000 Beta 0.50 Sunday, Apr. 04 - 03:47 PM EDT

     WorldTime2000 is a small utility for simultaneously viewing many different time zones from around the world. It is useful for anybody who has ever needed to know what the current time is in another part of the world. You can compare times from as many time zones as your display will allow. WorldTime 2000 is great for those who travel or plan to travel, have business dealings on a national or international level, or have family who live long distances away. Here's a rundown of features added in Beta 0.50:

  • Implementation of Daylight Savings.
  • Added over 350 locations.
  • More customizable. Spinning globe may be turned off, as well as 12 or 24 hour time.
  • 30 minute offsets from GMT has been implemented.

New Release Of IE 5 Sunday, Apr. 04 - 03:36 PM EDT

     It seems Microsoft has slipped a fast one on us, updating Internet Explorer 5.0 without a big press bonanza. Hundreds of people have written in, explaining how to go about downloading the new version, so we are going to let you all in on the secret. First you must delete your current IE 5 setup, because it will not find the updated components. Next, re-download the Active Setup file and you will find IE 5 and Outlook Express need to updated. The new build number is 5.00.2314.1003, as opposed to 5.00.2014.0216 in the March 18th release. We have not yet received any confirmation from Microsoft on what exactly has been changed, although a few users have written in noting that a bug with IE 5's clipboard had been fixed. If you have some bandwidth to spare, we recommend upgrading, although until we get the official word from Microsoft on the new release, all of you modem users might want to hold off.

DirectX Testers Sent Windows 2000 Friday, Apr. 02 - 09:54 PM EST

K+K Research Announces Music Publisher 01 Thursday, Apr. 01 - 06:36 PM EST

     K+K Research sends word that they have officially released Music Publisher 01. Music Publisher 01 is a software package for compressing music utilizing the latest AAC (MPEG-2 Advanced Audio Coding) technology, which gives full CD-quality. The MP01 package consists of a high-quality AAC encoder , a frontend for the encoder with batch-encoding and an AAC plug-in for Winamp, a versatile multi-format audio player. The sound quality of K+K AAC technology is the best AAC so far, offering better quality than Liquifier Pro (with Fraunhofer AAC), and gives CD quality at a bitrate of 128 kbps (audio transparency which makes it impossible to tell the AAC-file from the master WAV-file). A Demo version of MP01 AAC Encoder will be available to music publishers, other professional users and webmasters upon request.
