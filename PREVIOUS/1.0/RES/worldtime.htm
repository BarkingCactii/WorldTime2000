3 April, 1999

Welcome to the WorldTime2000 Beta page

WorldTime2000 should be useful for anybody who has ever needed to know what the current time is in another part of the world. You can compare times from as many time zones as your display will allow.

Great for those who travel or plan to travel, have business dealings on a national or international level, or have family who live long distances.

How to use

The last field shows the difference in hours from the home time zone. This is usually home, but can be any destination simply by clicking on another time location. If another location has been selected, it will be shown in red

Time Zones can be customized to be any name you wish. You can name Time Zones by family members, or a business, instead of simply by city.

To remove a location from the display, right click over the location to be removed, The location isn't removed from the list of locations, and can be added back at a later date.
