WorldTime2000 06-04-99
PCM Computer Systems Pty Ltd

H O T  C L I C K S

Weekly Top 10

PC Magazine utilities

  
 
WorldTime2000 is a good-looking app that can simultaneously display time information for many locations around the world. Instead of littering up your desktop with multiple clocks, it lists your desired cities and time zones in one centralized window, complete with a customizable background and an optional spinning globe. You can instantly see the hour difference between "there" and your home time zone. Click on a different location and WorldTime2000 automatically calculates hourly differences from the new reference point. The program includes time details for more than 400 cities and time zone; you can easily modify them and add more. You can also set audio alarms for any location. Despite a couple of rough edges, WorldTime2000 is one of the better global clock utilities we've seen.
Reviewed on Jun 04 1999.
